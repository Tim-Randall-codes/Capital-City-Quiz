CCQ 

Each page has a question text and four options, which are four buttons underneath. 
If the answer is correct a global variable saves one.
If incorrect a global variable saves one. 

There is a widget at the top of each page that will show the correct and incorrect answers. 

After the last question (page9) page10 will have the score, a well done, or try harder msg based 
On the score and play again button.
The play again button will reset the correct and incorrect variables back down to zero and take the
User back to page1.
